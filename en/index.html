Scheme of work with our company

1

Filling in the feedback form

You describe the task in general terms.

2

Sending the technical specification (T3)

After a general description of the task you send a detailed terms of reference (T3), which includes all the necessary requirements and wishes for the work.

3

discussion of terms, conditions, nuances

Our team studies the provided T3, estimates the scope of work and determines the timeframe for the project. Then we contact you to discuss the terms of cooperation, sign the contract and agree each stage of the work.

*In case of delivery of complex equipment and high-tech lines always from the manufacturer come specialists to supervise installation, setup, start-up and training.
